## 1. Design Direction

The Soul Healing website blends three core aesthetics drawn from the client-provided reference images:

1. **Dark Luxury Wellness** (from "Serenity" reference) - Rich, dark backgrounds (#1A1A1A / #2D2418) with warm cream and gold typography. Intimate, moody photography with circular image masks. This is the **dominant aesthetic** for the site.

2. **Lush Nature Retreat** (from "The Oasis" and waterfall references) - Full-bleed nature photography featuring waterfalls, dense greenery, and tropical foliage. Creates a sense of escape, grounding, and return to nature. Used for hero sections and transitional moments.

3. **Bold Modern Energy** (from "UTOPIA" reference) - Geometric layouts, the orange + green palette in action, and tropical plant illustrations. Brings the "loud, different, stand out" brand persona to life. Used sparingly for accent sections and interactive elements.

### Design Principles
- **Grounding over flashy** - the design should feel like a deep breath, not a sales pitch
- **Dark-first** - primary backgrounds are dark; light sections provide breathing room
- **Nature as texture** - greenery and water imagery woven throughout, not just as decoration
- **Elegant simplicity** - generous whitespace, intentional hierarchy, minimal clutter
- **Accessible warmth** - high contrast text on backgrounds, readable at all sizes

## 3. Page Layouts

### 3.1 Home Page

  - Example: "Welcome to Soul Healing, where your journey to transformation begins. Reconnect with peace, and embrace self-care."
- **CTA Button:** "Book Your Appointment"
  - Style: Bordered button, Warm Cream border + text, hover fills with Warm Orange

#### Featured Services Section
- **Background:** #1A1A1A (deep charcoal)
- **Section label:** "FEATURED SERVICES" - Montserrat, 12px, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 2px, Warm Cream
- **Description:** Montserrat, 16px, centered, max-width 640px, White

- **Image:** Portrait or lifestyle photo with dark overlay

## 7. Photography & Media Guide

### Required Photography
1. **Hero/banner images** - Waterfall scenes, dense lush forests, tropical greenery (2-3 images)
2. **Wellness/lifestyle** - Women in moments of calm, meditation, self-care (3-5 images)
3. **Professional portrait** - Natasha Daniels headshot/branding portrait (1-2 images)
4. **Nature close-ups** - Leaf textures, water surfaces, lotus flowers, moss (4-6 images for backgrounds/textures)
5. **Abstract/mood** - Soft light through trees, golden hour glow, misty environments (2-3 images)

### Photo Treatment Specifications
- **Color grading:** Warm tones, slightly lowered highlights, lifted shadows for a soft matte look
- **Contrast:** Medium - avoid harsh blacks or blown whites
- **Saturation:** Slightly muted greens, warm golden tones
- **Shadows:** Deep but not crushed, with warm brown undertones
- **Overall mood:** Intimate, grounding, sanctuary-like

### Stock Photo Sources
- Unsplash (nature/wellness)
- Pexels (lifestyle/wellness)
- Custom photography (preferred for Natasha's portraits and branded content)

## 8. Responsive Design

### Breakpoints
| Breakpoint | Width | Target |
|------------|-------|--------|
| Mobile | 0 - 767px | Phones |
| Tablet | 768px - 1023px | Tablets, small laptops |
| Desktop | 1024px - 1439px | Standard laptops/desktops |
| Large Desktop | 1440px+ | Wide monitors |

### Mobile-First Adjustments
- **Navigation:** Hamburger menu with full-screen overlay (dark background, centered links)
- **Hero:** Stack content vertically, reduce heading to 36px, increase CTA button size for touch
- **Service cards:** Single column stack
- **Values grid:** Single column stack (image then text)
- **Blog grid:** Single column
- **Images:** Full-width with maintained aspect ratios
- **Buttons:** Full-width on mobile for easy tapping
- **Typography:** Scale down headings by ~30%, maintain body at 16px minimum

### Touch Targets
- Minimum 44x44px for all interactive elements
- Adequate spacing between links in navigation
- Swipeable carousel for testimonials on mobile

## 9. Animations & Interactions

### Scroll Animations (Subtle)
- **Fade-in-up:** Content sections fade in and slide up 20px on scroll into view
- **Duration:** 600ms, ease-out
- **Stagger:** 100ms delay between sequential elements

### Hover States
- **Buttons:** Color shift (darken primary, fill outlined), 200ms transition
- **Cards:** Subtle Y-axis lift (translateY -4px) + soft shadow, 300ms transition
- **Links:** Underline slides in from left, 200ms
- **Images:** Slight scale (1.02) within container overflow hidden, 400ms

### Page Transitions
- Simple crossfade between pages (300ms) if using SPA framework
- Avoid heavy/distracting animations - maintain the serene, grounding feel

## 11. Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] All text meets WCAG AA contrast ratios (4.5:1 for body, 3:1 for large text)
- [ ] All images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Focus states visible on all interactive elements
- [ ] Navigation accessible via keyboard
- [ ] Form inputs properly labeled
- [ ] Color is not the sole indicator of meaning
- [ ] Animations respect prefers-reduced-motion
- [ ] Font sizes minimum 16px for body text

## 12. Technical Recommendations

### Platform
- Recommended: Wix, Squarespace, or WordPress with Elementor (based on client needs for blog + shop)
- Ensure booking/scheduling integration (Calendly, Acuity, or native platform booking)

### Performance
- Optimize all images (WebP format where supported)
- Lazy-load images below the fold
- Target < 3 second load time on mobile

### SEO Foundations
- Unique title tags and meta descriptions per page
- Schema markup for Local Business and Health/Wellness services
- Blog posts with proper heading hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)
- Alt text on all images

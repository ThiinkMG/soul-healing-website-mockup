## 1. Brand Overview

### Business Name
- **Primary Brand Name:** Soul Healing
- **Legal / Full Name:** Healing for the Soul Therapy & Training

### Mission Statement
To empower women through various healing modalities such as clinical hypnotherapy, trauma therapy, mindfulness, and to provide training on how to heal and nurture one's soul.

### Vision Statement
To provide evidence-based and trauma-focused training to various institutions and communities.

## 2. Brand Persona & Voice

### Brand Personality
Soul Healing is **loud, different, innovative, and unapologetically bold.** The brand stands out in the wellness and therapy space by combining clinical expertise with a warm, community-driven approach.

### Brand Metaphor
> "Healing like Ginger Ale - spicy, tangy, and self-healing."

This captures the essence of the brand: healing that has depth, a bit of edge, and empowers the individual to become their own source of restoration.

### Brand Attributes
- **Impactful** - Every touchpoint should feel meaningful and transformative
- **Community-Oriented** - Built on connection, listening, and understanding
- **Strategic** - Evidence-based, intentional, and results-driven
- **Innovative** - Breaking the mold of traditional therapy aesthetics
- **Empowering** - Centering women's strength and capacity for healing

### Brand Voice
| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Speak with warmth and authority | Use cold clinical jargon |
| Be direct and empowering | Be preachy or condescending |
| Use language of transformation | Use language of brokenness |
| Be culturally sensitive and inclusive | Generalize experiences |
| Encourage rest and self-compassion | Promote "hustle" or "push through" culture |

### Key Messaging Pillars
1. **"If there's life, there is hope."** - The brand's foundational philosophy
2. **Subconscious Rewiring** - Lasting change through neural pathway healing, not temporary relief
3. **Permission to Rest** - Women can rest and do not have to carry it all
4. **Safe Space** - No judgement, collaborative interaction, empathic listening
5. **Efficient Healing** - People don't have to spend years in therapy

## 3. Logo

### Primary Logo Mark
The Soul Healing logo is a continuous-line illustration combining three symbolic elements requested by the client:

- **Hummingbird** - Represents resilience, joy, and the ability to move through life with lightness despite hardship
- **Heart** - Symbolizes love, compassion, emotional healing, and the heart-centered approach to therapy
- **Lotus** - Represents growth through adversity, purity, spiritual awakening, and transformation

The hummingbird forms the upper portion of the mark, flowing downward into an intertwined heart and lotus at the base. The continuous-line style conveys unity, wholeness, and the interconnected nature of mind-body-soul healing.

### Logo Files
- **`Soul Healing - Logo 1- Clean.png`** — Full logo with "SOUL HEALING / Therapy & Training" wordmark
- **`Soul Healing - Logo 1- Monogram-notext.png`** — Monogram/icon mark only (no text)

### Logo Color — Green Monochrome (Primary)
- **Outline/Stroke:** Deep Forest Green (#1A6B35)
- **Fill/Body:** Light Sage Green (#C5E1A5 / #B8D98E)
- **Wordmark:** Deep Forest Green (#1A6B35) — italic serif "SOUL HEALING", regular weight "Therapy & Training"
- *Best for:* All primary brand usage — website, social media, print, merch

#### White (Reversed)
- Full mark in solid white (#FFFFFF)
- *Best for:* Dark backgrounds, overlays on photography

#### Black (Monochrome)
- Full mark in solid black (#000000)
- *Best for:* Single-color printing, fax, embossing, watermarks

### Logo Usage Rules
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the lotus element
- Minimum size: 40px height for digital, 0.5" height for print
- Never stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
- Never place the color logo on a busy/competing background without a container or overlay
- The monochrome versions should be used when the color versions do not have sufficient contrast

## 5. Typography

### Heading Font - Playfair Display (or similar elegant serif)
- Used for: Page titles, section headings, hero text, pull quotes
- Style: Bold and Italic variants
- Conveys: Elegance, authority, luxury, warmth

### Body Font - Montserrat (or similar clean sans-serif)
- Used for: Body copy, descriptions, navigation, buttons, captions
- Style: Regular (400) and Semi-Bold (600)
- Conveys: Modern, clean, approachable, readable

## 8. Imagery & Photography Direction

### Photography Style
- **Warm, grounding tones** - golden hour warmth, amber highlights, earthy shadows
- **Nature-forward** - lush greenery, waterfalls, forests, tropical foliage
- **Intimate and serene** - close-up textures, water droplets, soft light through leaves
- **Wellness and self-care** - moments of rest, meditation, self-connection
- **Diverse representation** - centering black and brown women in all lifestyle imagery

### Mood References
The following themes from the reference images should guide all visual content:
1. **"Serenity" aesthetic** - Dark, luxurious backgrounds with warm gold/cream tones and intimate wellness photography
2. **"The Oasis" aesthetic** - Lush waterfall environments with a sense of retreat and escape
3. **"UTOPIA" aesthetic** - Bold green and orange geometric compositions with tropical plant life
4. **Nature textures** - Close-up leaf veins, flowing water, aerial forest views, moss and stone

### Image Treatment
- Slightly desaturated warmth with deep shadows
- Dark vignetting for moody, intimate feel
- Overlay text in cream/gold on dark imagery
- Circular crop masks for profile and service images (referencing "Serenity" design)
